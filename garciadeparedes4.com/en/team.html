Team

To achieve excellence in every project, Grosvenor makes local decisions based on the unique characteristics of each neighbourhood and with respect for the people who live within them. One of the most important decisions to make is the choice of specialists who best know the local area – specialists that put their experience and dedication at the forefront of every project and who, of course, share the philosophy of Grosvenor.

CANO Y ESCARIO was formed in 1988 by architects Benjamín Cano Domínguez and Diego Escario Travesedo. 

The work of CANO Y ESCARIO maintains a rounded, integrated and individual architectural language. They are specialists in the refurbishment of buildings and residential homes.

 

The founders define their work as architecture which is balanced, responsible, capable, intelligent, substantial, serene, long-lasting, relevant and striking.
