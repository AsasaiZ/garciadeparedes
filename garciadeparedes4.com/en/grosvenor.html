From its early beginnings, developing the elegant London neighbourhoods of Mayfair in the 18th century and Belgravia in the 19th century, to the present day, Grosvenor has developed projects in the most sought-after neighbourhoods around the world, and it has added spectacular contributions to the skylines of cities from Vancouver to Tokyo and Washington to Hong Kong.

Living Madrid.
Living Cities.

The Living Cities philosophy of Grosvenor reflects its commitment to contribute to the economic, social and environmental well-being of urban communities. This goal steers each project from its very conception in the hope of improving not only the lives of those who inhabit it, but also those around it. Now and in future generations.

Through its various projects, Grosvenor is committed to creating architectural icons of timeless quality that help to develop dynamic communities around them.

More than creating buildings, its goal is to create unique experiences and lives for the people who inhabit them.
