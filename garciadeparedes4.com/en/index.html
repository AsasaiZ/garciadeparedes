Project

The meeting of two centuries


García de Paredes 4 is a new residential concept where traditional and modernity come together. Located in the heart of Chamberí, the building stands in one of the most exclusive and up and coming neighbouroods in Madrid.

17 unique homes, designed by the prestigious architecture studio Cano y Escario, configure the new project by Grosvenor, characterised by the highest standards of sustainability and construction.

Building

The building

The building, which holds more than 70 years of history, comprises a total of six floors and will be divided by two unique designs. Its iconic mid-20th century façade of the first three floors will be preserved by meticulous restoration work. Traditional elements will be conserved including the handcrafted detail of the plasterwork and cast-iron balcony railings, whilst maintaining the building´s original splendour.


In contrast to the original building, the top three floors will stand out for their brand-new contemporary design, creating a balanced blend between tradition and modernity.

 

Communal facilities

Communal facilities

The residents´ facilities of García de Paredes 4 have been designed with the aim of offering exclusive services similar to those which one might expect in residential areas outside of the city centre. One of the distinctive amenities is the spacious rooftop terrace with swimming pool; the perfect place from which to observe the skyline of Madrid:


  • Rooftop terrace and swimming pool
  • Gym with contemporary equipment
  • Spacious entrance lobby with multipurpose area
  • Room for courier deliveries
  • Private garage with facilities to charge electric vehicles
  • Individual storage rooms allocated per home
  • Bike storage



Sustainability

Sustainability

In line with Grosvenor´s long-term commitment to a more sustainable future, García de Paredes 4 seeks to optimise the energetic efficiency in each phase of the project. In this way the building has been awarded a Class A energy efficiency certificate (the maximum score in energy consumption) and is in the process of obtaining a “Very Good” rating from the prestigious BREEAM® organisation, the highest international recognition in sustainable construction. Thanks to this, the residents of García de Paredes 4 will be able to save between 50% and 70% on their energy bills and up to 40% on water consumption compared with traditional homes (source: BREEAM®).

Homes

Homes

Tailor-made homes


17 contemporary and flexible homes comprising 1 and 2 bedrooms, including 2 fantastic penthouses with individual terraces. The homes have a flexible layout and and are designed with precision to optimise the use of space and maximise the entry of natural light.


Quality and comfort

From the exterior to the interior of the building, the high standards of quality convert García de Paredes 4 in a referent for quality and comfort. All the homes are equipped with the latest technological advances in home automation and, radiant heating/cooling floor and contemporary kitchens with premium electro domestic appliances.


The personalisation, selection of premium brands and the adaptation to the latest trends, convert the homes of García de Paredes 4 in unique spaces where comfort emanates from every step.

Location

The charm of Chamberí


García de Paredes 4 enjoys a privileged location in the Canal area within the popular Chamberí district. A privileged location in one of the most in demand districts of Madrid.

To live in García de Paredes 4 is to live amongst its tree-lined streets filled with centuries of history, its majestic buildings, and incomparable offer of leisure and cosmopolitan gastronomy.

CANAL

In close proximity to the Santander Park, Canal Theatres and the Plaza of Olavide. Along with different public transport stations which allows an excellent connection with the rest of the capital city. The residents of García de Paredes 4 will be able to enjoy long strolls amongst tree-lined streets and historic facades, to watch an exposition in the Sala Canal or to enjoy some tapas in Ponzano, one of the most fashionable gastronomic streets in Madrid.

CHAMBERÍ

To live in Chamberí is to be a neighbour to the arts, leisure, traditional and cosmopolitan dining, the theatre, green spaces, and the best options of public and private transport.
